Kai Koenig Software Solutions Architect, Ventego Creative

I work as a Software Solutions Architect for Ventego Creative in Wellington, New Zealand.

I co-founded the company with two partners and I'm also the CTO of Zen Ex Machina, a startup in the fields of digital & user experience consultancy based out of Canberra in Australia. My work really comprises a mix of consulting, training, mentoring and actual development work using a range of technologies, common themes being Java, Android, CFML, JavaScript etc.

I'm well versed in Java and some other JVM-based languages like Clojure or Groovy and recently (re-)discovered the pleasure of writing software in Python and Go. Kotlin has been my language love for the last few years though.

Other stuff I occasionally do:

  • Write for magazines (currently mainly Heise's iX in Germany) or in my Blog
  • Publish a Podcast (2 Developers Down Under) with my friend Mark Mandel from Melbourne and run New Zealand's first Kotlin meetup (Wellington.kt)
  • Since 2007 I fly small, single-engine airplanes around New Zealand and sometimes Australia, currently working on my Commercial Pilot License.
